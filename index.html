About Me

I'm Katie. Alabama born and living in Manhattan. I'm passionate about my people, education, travel, and creating things. I am a teacher by day and a creative all the time.

Educator

I've been teaching for seven years, in both public and private schools, spending most of those years in fifth grade.

Artist

Around five years ago, I taught myself how to hand letter using a form of faux caligraphy. I have ventured into creating large and small scale projects. /p>

Content Creator

Being so far away from family and other loved ones, I started a YouTube channel to share my experiences in a new city. This spurred a new hobby and interest - vlogging and creating videos.

Skills and Services

I'm always up for a new project. Below you will find some of the things I've done up to this point. Let's chat about stuff we can work on together!

Lettering

I have created and lettered signs, invitations, prints, baby announcements, and home decor. I've also recently started exploring digital lettering and vector assets.

Videos

I've been vlogging mainly about life in a new place, but my various interests slip in from time to time. Makeup and skincare are a huge interest of mine.

Blogging

Writing has always been therapeutic for me. Sharing experiences helps us all grow.
